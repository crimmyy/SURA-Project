The SURA Project.

Exploring how the Village Sam in Chiang Mai adapts to globalization. By combining archival research with interactive animation, we explore the impact of these modern developments on traditional socio-ecological practices....

Getting Started

Archival Research

To become versed, various stories and readings about Chiang Mai, the Mae Kuang, and the Village Sam were explored to craft a narrative combining Magical Realism with the current socio-ecological state of Chiang Mai.

Learning the Works

Three.js + Blender

Skills in Three.js and Blender were developed to envision the project. Practice included modeling a watercolor piece by Cheng Kai-Hsiang from 2D to 3D.

Storyboarding

Drawing! + More Reading

Additional brainstorming and reading helped grasp the topics to create a personal narrative, focusing on the Village Sam which was a valuable practice before development.

Development

Workshopping + Testing

Played around, made mistakes, broke stuff. Experimentation led to valuable learning experiences, including Tailwind CSS and Alpine JS for functionality and efficiency, as well as making significant advancements with Blender.
